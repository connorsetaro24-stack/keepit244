The Founder's Story

From the Darkest Round to the Brightest Corner

Every fighter has a story. The founder of alive@keep;t 24 knows what it feels like to be knocked down by life — the weight of addiction, the suffocating grip of depression, the loneliness that comes when you think nobody understands.

But through faith, brotherhood, and the relentless decision to keep fighting, a new chapter began. alive@keep;t 24 was born from that fire — a movement to help men find their feet again, to build each other up, and to prove that no round is the last round.

"I didn't just survive. I chose to be alive — and I want that for every brother out there."
